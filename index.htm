Hiba Nawab

Actress

About me

Hiba Nawab is an Indian television actress known for her portrayal of Anushka in Channel V India‘s Crazy Stupid Ishq and Amaya in Star Plus‘s Tere Sheher Mein

Hiba Nawab was a child artist in Saat Phere, Lo Ho Gayi Pooja Iss Ghar Ki and Ssshhhh…Phir Koi Hai.
In 2015, Nawab portrayed Amaya Mathur in Star Plus‘s Tere Sheher Mein. She also sang the reprised version of Dheere Dheere.
She played Elaichi Bansal in SAB TV‘s Jijaji Chhat Per Hain for which she won the Indian Telly Award for Best Actor In Comic Role Female Popular.

Tere Sheher Mein

Tere Sheher Mein is an Indian television drama, which premiered on 2 March 2015 and aired on Star Plus and went off air on 14 November 2015.The show starred Hiba Nawab, Dhruv Bhandari, Anjum Fakih, Gautami Kapoor, Isha Mishra, Sana Amin Sheikh, Kinshuk Mahajan and S. M. Zaheer. It was produced by Rajan Shahi's Director's Kut Productions.Some early episodes of the show were shot in Paris and Mumbai.[6] The show was set in Varanasi.

Kuch Smiles Ho Jayein..

Kuch Smiles Ho Jayein... With Alia is an Indian comedy series which was made at home during the lockdown. It was created by Aayush Agrawal during 2020 COVID-19 quarantine, it was completely shot remotely, edited and aired on Sony SAB. It was hosted by Alia(Anusha Mishra) from Tera Kya Hoga Alia and Balraj Syal from Apna News Aayega. The series ended on 1 June 2020 after airing 5 episodes

Jijaji Chhat Per Hain

Jijaji Chhat Per Hain (transl. Brother-in-law is on the terrace) is an Indian Sitcom Television serial that first time premiered on 9 January 2018 and concluded on 21 February 2020 on Sony SAB. The show involves Murari (Anup Upadhyay), who continually tries to control his daughter, Elaichi (Hiba Nawab), a street smart and free spirited girl; and Pancham (Nikhil Khurana), an aspiring music director, Who works as a salesman in Murari's (Anup Upadhyay) Lehenga shop, and is in love with Elaichi. It is produced by Edit II Production.

Crazy Stupid Ishq

Crazy Stupid Ishq is an Indian television youth drama show which premiered on 27 May 2013 on Channel V India. Produced by Sphere Origins, it starred Hiba Nawab, Aneri Vajani, Harsh Rajput and Vishal Vashishtha

Bhaag Bakool Bhaag

Bhaag Bakool Bhaag (English : Run Bakool Run) is an Indian Hindi television sitcom, which premiered on 15 May 2017 and was broadcast on Colors TV,[citation needed] The series is produced by Edit II Productions of Sanjay Kohli. The series aired every Monday-Friday. In The UK it was broadcast on Rishtey Europe.
